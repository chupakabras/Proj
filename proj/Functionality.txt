Final program functionality

Program creates online gift shop where clients can buy and order different gifts.
Every gift is limited, program shows how many gifts can be bought and how many already left.
Then someone "enters" the online shop it asks to type your name and email to identify 
what gifts you have already bought, what you need to order and what you will buy now.
Online shop has two modes: for buying and ordering gifts. Then someone orders the gift program reserves chosen time for that client.
